```{prf:definition} Reciprocal basis
In a inner product space, the reciprocal basis of a given basis $\{ \vec{b}_a \}_{a=1:d}$ is the set of vectors $\{ \vec{b}_{b} \}_{b=1:d}$, s.t.

$$\vec{b}^b \cdot \vec{b}_a = \delta_a^b \ .$$

```
